## 🏗️ Architecture Knowledge

<table>
<tr>
<td valign="top" width="50%">

### 🧩 Microservices

A **modular system design** — small, **independent services** each handling a specific business function.

- ✅ Independent deployment & scaling
- ✅ Docker, Kubernetes, REST/GraphQL
- ✅ Great for large distributed systems

</td>
<td valign="top" width="50%">

### 🏛️ Monolithic

A **single unified codebase** — frontend, backend, and database operate as one system.

- ✅ Simpler to develop & deploy
- ✅ Ideal for MVPs & small teams
- ✅ Can evolve into microservices later

</td>
</tr>
</table>
